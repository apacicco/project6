Pacicco&Pacicco Jewelers, nestled in the heart of New York City, boasts a rich history dating back to 1948 when it was founded by the Caccioli family. With a heritage spanning over seven decades, this iconic jewelry store has earned a reputation for excellence and a deep-rooted commitment to craftsmanship. The Pacicco family's unwavering dedication to quality and creativity has made them a trusted name in the world of fine jewelry. Over the years, they have catered to discerning clientele, including celebrities and socialites, by offering a unique blend of classic elegance and contemporary design. Their meticulously curated collection of diamond, gemstone, and precious metal jewelry has made Pacicco&Pacicco a landmark destination for those seeking enduring beauty and sophistication in the heart of New York City.

The mission of Pacicco&Pacicco Jewelers is to celebrate life's most precious moments by crafting exquisite, one-of-a-kind pieces that transcend generations. Their artisans bring forth a fusion of timeless artistry and cutting-edge techniques, ensuring that each creation is a masterpiece that tells a unique story. Beyond their commitment to exceptional design, the Pacicco family is dedicated to providing exceptional service and forging lasting relationships with their clientele. They understand that purchasing jewelry is a deeply personal experience, and they take pride in guiding customers through the process, helping them select or even custom-design pieces that reflect their individuality and emotions. In doing so, Pacicco&Pacicco Jewelers has not only become a prominent fixture in the New York jewelry scene but also an institution that embodies the essence of luxury, craftsmanship, and enduring beauty.
